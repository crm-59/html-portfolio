The Best Movies According to Carlos

My 5 top Movies of All-Time.

Black Hawk Down

Great historical movie about a rescue in Mogadishu.

Fargo

Movie about a kidnapping gone wrong in Fargo, North Dakota.

The French Connection

Great movie about drug contraband from France to New york.

The Godfather

Great movie about the American Mafia.

Kingdom of Heaven

Great historical movie about some of the events in Jerusalem during the Crusades.
